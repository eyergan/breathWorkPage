Can Breathwork Help?

With some things, yes! Not everything.

You're (likely) already breathing! While some types of breathwork are intense and take a long time to master, others are simple and can be practiced anytime. Simple breathwork techniques often help people with things like stress, focus, nervous system regulation, sleep and waking up!!
